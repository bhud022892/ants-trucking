About

The Act Now Trucking Service was founded in 2010 by Tammy and Steve Schoen. We have been leaders in the truck transportation industry for many years. We started with a high concept of customer service through out the industry. The team consists of professional truckers and an office staff with the mind set of “no job is too big or too small”. We run a background check and conduct random drug screens on all of our drivers.
